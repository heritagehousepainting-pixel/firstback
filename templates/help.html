Getting started

Setup & onboarding

How long does setup take?

Most contractors are live within a day. You add your business details, point your missed calls at FirstBack, and run one test conversation.

Do I need any equipment or apps?

No. FirstBack works with the phone number you already have — there’s nothing to install on a customer’s phone and no new hardware.

Can I change how it talks later?

Any time. Update your AI instructions in Settings and the change applies to new conversations immediately.

Calls & forwarding

How calls reach FirstBack

Can I keep my existing number?

Yes. You forward only unanswered calls to FirstBack and keep the number your customers already know.

What happens on a missed call?

FirstBack texts the caller within seconds, answers their questions in your voice, and books an estimate from the windows you’ve made available.

How are urgent jobs handled?

Emergency language is detected automatically, the lead is flagged, and you’re notified right away — the conversation still continues with the caller.

Billing & plans

Pricing & account

Are there per-call fees?

No. Every plan is a flat monthly rate with no per-call or per-minute charges. See Pricing for details.

Can I cancel anytime?

Yes — no contracts and no cancellation fees. Cancelling stops future billing.

How do I upgrade or change plans?

Switch plans from your account at any time; changes take effect on your next billing cycle.
