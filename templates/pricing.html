FAQ

Questions, answered.

Are there per-call or per-minute fees?

No. Your plan is one flat monthly rate — answer as many calls as your plan allows with no metered charges or surprise overages.

Will it text spam callers or my existing customers?

No. FirstBack screens every missed call first — it texts back real prospects, skips spam and robocalls (which protects your number from being flagged), and leaves people you already know to you. It builds that “known” list automatically as you book jobs, so there’s nothing to import. And it’s precision-first: anything uncertain is still texted, never silenced.

Can FirstBack call customers back by voice, not just text?

Coming soon. Voice callback is in beta and rolling out on Pro and Crew. When it ships, a customer who would rather talk can ask and FirstBack will place an AI voice call that books the estimate -- only after the customer asks, and never outside quiet hours. Today FirstBack handles everything by text.

Can I keep my existing phone number?

Yes. You forward your unanswered calls to FirstBack and keep the number your customers already know.

How long does setup take?

Setup is same-day: we train FirstBack on your business and help you forward your missed calls, so call screening and forwarding work right away. Live text-back to your customers switches on once your carrier registration (A2P) clears — usually 1–5 business days. That carrier step is industry-wide for any business texting service, and we handle the paperwork for you.

What if I want to cancel?

Cancel anytime — there are no contracts and no cancellation fees.
