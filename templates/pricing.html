FAQ

Questions, answered.

Are there per-call or per-minute fees?

No. Your plan is one flat monthly rate — answer as many calls as your plan allows with no metered charges or surprise overages.

Can RingBack call customers back by voice, not just text?

Yes. When a customer would rather talk, RingBack places an AI voice call that speaks with them and books the estimate — included on Pro and Crew. It only calls after the customer asks, and never outside quiet hours.

Can I keep my existing phone number?

Yes. You forward your unanswered calls to RingBack and keep the number your customers already know.

How long does setup take?

Most contractors are live within a day. We train RingBack on your business and help you forward missed calls.

What if I want to cancel?

Cancel anytime — there are no contracts and no cancellation fees.
