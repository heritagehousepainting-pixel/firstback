Component system

The shared token + component layer every product screen is built from. Approve this and the Dashboard, Settings, and Simulator get rebuilt on top of it. Hover / active / focus states are shown forced here so you can see the whole matrix at once — they also fire on real interaction.

Tokens · Color

Neutrals do ~90% of the work; the accent is a scalpel. Stars are amber, never the accent.
